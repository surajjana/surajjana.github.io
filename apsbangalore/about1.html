About Aryan Public School


Through a caring and supportive school environment, we foster in our students a love of learning and a sense of belonging. Our school maintains many of its proud traditions but embraces new technologies and approaches to ensure that all students reach their academic, physical and social potential both now and into the future.

Aryan public School achieves outstanding results through its range of high-quality and inclusive academic, sporting and cultural programs. Our staff and community are committed to the provision of a variety of activities and programs that enable our students to become well-rounded and confident individuals.

  Aryan public School benefits from a supportive and vibrant community who work together to ensure that all students achieve their potential and excel through their journey .

From the principal’s desk



As the local community saw the need for a strong vibrant school that would provide generations of students with a very good education - an education that focuses on the whole child.

Aryan public School was officially opened in 2014. The School has exceeded expectations and has built a reputation as a school of excellence. The facilities and programs have continually adapted to meet the changing educational needs of the many students who have attended our beautiful school.
As the principal to be given the honour of leading Aryan public School, I am committed to ensuring that our school remains at the forefront of educational excellence. We hold dear our proud traditions whilst embracing contemporary practices to ensure our every student is equipped to succeed now and into the future.

I am confident in the quality and breadth of education that every child receives at Aryan . In keeping with our school motto – excel through knowledge our students exit our school as confident, lifelong learners, well-prepared to meet their next challenge and excel in all the fields now and in the future.

I welcome you and your family to our school. We are fortunate to have a supportive school community who work tirelessly to enhance students’ educational experiences. Your first impression of our school will undoubtedly be the strong sense of community that exists. I encourage you to become involved in our school to help maintain this great community spirit. I am extremely proud to be the principal of this great school.


Shashank Mane H

Principal
