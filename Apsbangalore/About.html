About us


ARYAN PUBLIC School is a highly sought after primary school located in singanayakanahalli, an outer-city suburb of bangalore. The school, established in 2014, boasts beautiful mix of everything in creating a very good study atmosphere for the students.

Through a caring and supportive school environment, we foster in our students a love of learning and a sense of belonging. Our school maintains many of its proud traditions but embraces new technologies and approaches to ensure that all students reach their academic, physical and social potential both now and into the future.Aryan public School achieves outstanding results through its range of high-quality and inclusive academic, sporting and cultural programs. Our staff and community are committed to the provision of a variety of activities and programs that enable our students to become well-rounded and confident individuals.

Aryan public School benefits from a supportive and vibrant community who work together to ensure that all students achieve their potential and excel through their journey .
